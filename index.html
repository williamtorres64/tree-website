Importance of Water

Water is essential for all known forms of life. It covers over 70% of Earth's surface and plays a vital role in regulating our climate.

Uses of Water

Water is used for drinking, agriculture, industry, sanitation, and recreation. It is a critical resource in our daily lives.

Water Conservation

Conserving water helps ensure there is enough for future generations. Simple actions like fixing leaks and using water-efficient appliances can make a big difference.
